Lotus Flower

Lotus Flower is a classic flower in Origami. It's the prettiest thing that origami have done.


What do you need:
Square paper
Clips

Steps:

  1. Prepare to apply a horizontal valley-fold and then unfold.
  2. Apply the horizontal valley-fold of the step #1 and then unfold. Prepare to apply the vertical valley-fold and then unfold.
  3. Apply the vertical valley-fold of the step #2 and then unfold.Prepare to apply a "cushion-fold" .
  4. Apply the first "cushion-fold" of the step #3. Prepare to apply a "cushion-fold" .
  5. Apply the second "cushion-fold" of the step #4.Prepare to apply a "cushion-fold" .
  6. Apply the third "cushion-fold" of the step #5.Prepare to turn the origami lotus flower model over.
  7. Turn the origami lotus flower model over.Prepare to apply a "cushion-fold" .
  8. Apply the fourth "cushion-fold" of the step #7.Prepare to apply four valley-folds.
  9. Apply four valley-folds of the step #8.Prepare to turn the origami lotus flower model over.
  10. Turn the origami lotus flower model over.Prepare to pull up the front-most layer of the upper-left corner.
  11. Start pulling up the front-most layer of the upper-left corner.
  12. Continue pulling up the front-most layer of the upper-left corner whiling rotating the origami lotus flower model.
  13. Keep pulling up the front-most layer of the upper-left corner whiling rotating the origami lotus flower model.
  14. Continue rotating the origami lotus flower model.
  15. Start lifting the petal while pressing the region below the petal using your left thumb.
  16. Continue lifting the petal while pressing the region below the petal using your left thumb.
  17. Keep lifting the petal while pressing the region below the petal using your left thumb.
  18. Finish lifting the petal while pressing the region below the petal using your left thumb.Prepare to repeat the steps #10-17 for other three petals.
  19. Repeat the steps #10-17 for other three petals.Prepare to turn the origami lotus flower model over.
  20. Turn the origami lotus flower model over.Prepare to pull the left petal up.
  21. Start pulling the left petal up.
  22. Continue pulling the left petal up while rotating the origami lotus flower model.
  23. Keep pulling the left petal up while rotating the origami lotus flower model.
  24. Finish pulling the left petal up while rotating the origami lotus flower model.Prepare to rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.
  25. Rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees to see the petal.
  26. Rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.Prepare to repeat the steps #21-25 for all three petals.
  27. Repeat the steps #21-25 for all three petalsPrepare to turn the origami lotus flower model over.
  28. Turn the origami lotus flower model over.Prepare to rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.
  29. Rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.Prepare to pull the left petal up.
  30. Start pulling the left petal up.Prepare to rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.
  31. Rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.Continue pulling the left petal up.
  32. Finish pulling the left petal up while rotating the origami lotus flower model.Prepare to turn the origami lotus flower model over.
  33. Turn the origami lotus flower model over.Prepare to rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.
  34. Rotate the origami lotus flower model by 90 degrees.Prepare to repeat the steps #30- 34 for three more times.
  35. Repeat the steps #30- 34 for three more times.Prepare to turn the origami lotus flower model over.
  36. Turn the origami lotus flower model over.Prepare to rotate the origami lotus flower model by 45 degrees.
  37. Rotate the origami lotus flower model by 45 degrees. Prepare to curl one outer petal.
  38. Curl one outer petal.Prepare to curl all four outer petals.
  39. Curl all four outer petals.Prepare to curl one middle petal.
  40. Curl one middle petal. Prepare to curl all four middle petals.
  41. Curl all four middle petals. Prepare to curl one inner petal.
  42. Curl one inner petal. Prepare to culr all four inner petals.
  43. Curl all four inner petals.
  44. Congratulation! You have successfully completed making a traditional origami lotus.

Origami Crane

Origami Crane is a classic figure in Origami.It's for beginner and you can DIY(Do It Yourself)


What do you need:
Square paper
Clips

Steps:

  1. Let's start with a paper's front side facing up.Prepare for a valley-fold along a diagonal line
  2. Apply the valley-fold of the step #1.
  3. Prepare for a valley-fold along another diagonal line.
  4. Valley-fold along the diagonal line.
  5. Prepare to mountain-fold along the middle horizontal line.
  6. Apply the mountain-fold of the step #5.
  7. Prepare to mountain-fold along the middle vertical line.
  8. Mountain-fold along the middle vertical line.Prepare to grab a point merging with two arrows with two fingers.Since there are two sets of two arrows, you need to use two hands.
  9. Grab two edges with fingers.Prepare to squeeze the model using the direction of given arrows.
  10. Squeeze the model
  11. Prepare to move two fingers following the arrow directions.
  12. Flatten the model.Prepare to rotate the model vertically by 90 degrees.
  13. Rotate the model vertically by 90 degrees.
  14. Lay the model down.Making of square base is now completed.We will work from this base to make another base called "bird base".
  15. First, make sure that the open side is at the lower half.Prepare for two valley-folds.
  16. Apply two valley-folds according to the step 15.Prepare for another valley-fold.
  17. Valley-fold according to the step #16.Prepare to unfold three previous valley-folds.
  18. Unfold all three valley-folds.
  19. Let's begin so-called petal-fold.Lift the bottom corner upwards.
  20. Continue lifting.
  21. As you push the corner up, bring the edges in towards the middle and flatten them.
  22. Now, you completed the petal-fold.
  23. Prepare to turn the model over.
  24. Turn the model over.Now, the situation is the same as the step #15.Prepare to repeat the steps #15-22.
  25. Repeat the steps #15-22.The bird base is now completed.
  26. Prepare for two valley-folds.
  27. Apply two valley-folds according to the step #26.Prepare to turn the model over.
  28. Turn the model over.Prepare for two valley-folds.Here we are repeating the step #26.
  29. Apply two valley-folds of the step #28.Prepare to valley-fold the right side down to the left side.
  30. Fold the right side down to the left side.Prepare to apply a so-called "inside reverse-fold" which consists of two valley-folds and one mountain-fold.
  31. Apply the inside reverse-fold of the step #30.Prepare to lay down the model.
  32. Lay the model down.Prepare to valley-fold the left side down to the right side.
  33. Valley-fold according to the step #32.Prepare to apply an inside reverse-fold.
  34. Inside reverse-fold according to the step #33.Prepare to squeeze the model.
  35. Flatten the model.The body structure is completed.Prepare to concentrate on "Head" part..
  36. Let's look at the model from the front.Prepare for another inside reverse-fold to make a head tof this crane.
  37. Apply the inside reverse-fold of the step #36.Prepare to lay down the model.
  38. Lay the model down.Prepare for a valley-fold.
  39. Valley-fold the right wing.Prepare to look into the left wing,
  40. Prepare to valley-fold the left wing also.
  41. The crane is now completed.
  42. If you want, you can make the body of the crane be expanded.Pull two wings apart in an opposite direction so that the body can be expanded.
  43. The crane with an expanded body is now completed.Congratulation!Traditional origami crane is now completed.
